Marketing Grants

The Diggers & Dealers Marketing Grants Program supports mining-focused organisations in boosting brand visibility, promoting innovations, and engaging stakeholders through high-impact marketing initiatives.

Grant Objectives

  • Support brand development and campaign execution
  • Fund marketing for new mining technologies and services
  • Enable production of digital and print media assets
  • Promote ESG, community, and indigenous outreach programs

Key Dates

  • Applications Open: 15 April 2025
  • Deadline: 30 June 2025
  • Notification: 15 August 2025
  • Awards Announced: Diggers & Dealers 2025 Forum

Application Requirements

  • Marketing Plan (max 3 pages)
  • Budget Breakdown
  • Existing Collateral (optional)
  • Target Audience Overview
  • Measurable Outcomes or KPIs
